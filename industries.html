Coal

High-Thermal Grade Coal

VN Trade's primary facilitation activity centres on high-thermal grade coal — facilitating physical transactions between exporters, trading companies and power or industrial buyers across global import corridors.

We work across the full range of high-thermal grades, from standard 5,500 kcal NAR to premium 6,300 kcal GAR, and facilitate trades on Handysize through Panamax vessels depending on cargo size.

Key Origins

Indonesia (Kalimantan), Australia (Hunter Valley / Queensland), South Africa (Richards Bay), Russia (Kuzbass). Primary export terminals: Balikpapan, Newcastle, RBCT.

Import Corridors

India (east and west coast ports), China, South Korea, Japan, Vietnam, Bangladesh, Pakistan — and power utilities across MENA and Sub-Saharan Africa.

Trade Basis

FOB, CFR and CIF. ASTM / ISO sampling and analysis. Pricing referenced to Argus McCloskey / Platts APAC indices. Surveyor: SGS / Cotecna at load and discharge.

Metals

Metals & Mining

We facilitate physical metals transactions — connecting steel mills, mining companies, trading houses and industrial end-users across the key iron ore, steel products and copper corridors.

Iron Ore

Lump, fines and pellet feed. Fe range 58 – 65%. Origins: Australia (Pilbara), Brazil (Carajas). Capesize / Panamax vessels. Pricing: Platts IODEX 62% Fe CFR China.

Steel Products

Steel Billets 3SP / 5SP, Hot-Rolled Coil (HRC) and Wire Rod. Origins: Turkey, CIS. Basis: FOB, CFR, CIF. Lot size: 5,000 – 30,000 MT. Spec: JIS G3108 / ASTM A1011.

Copper

Copper Cathode Grade A (BS EN 1978) and Copper Concentrate (25 – 30% Cu). Origins: Chile, Peru, DRC. Pricing: LME Cash / 3-Month. Lot size: 500 – 5,000 MT cathode.

Fertilizers

Fertilizers & Nutrients

Our fertilizer facilitation leads with nitrogen and phosphate products — Urea, DAP and NPK complexes — connecting Middle Eastern, Russian, North African and South American producers with importing markets across Asia and Africa.

Nitrogen Products

Granular and Prilled Urea (46% N), Ammonium Sulphate (21% N), Ammonium Nitrate. Origins: Middle East, Russia, Egypt, Algeria. Cargo: 5,000 – 50,000 MT, FOB / CFR / CIF.

Phosphate & Potash

DAP 18-46-0, NPK blends, MOP (Muriate of Potash). Origins: Morocco (OCP), Saudi Arabia, Canada, Belarus. Pricing: Argus / ICIS benchmark. IFA quality compliance.

Import Markets

India, Bangladesh, Pakistan, Vietnam, Myanmar — and emerging markets across Sub-Saharan Africa and Latin America where agricultural input demand is growing fastest.

Agri Products

Grains & Soft Commodities

Wheat, Corn, Soybeans and Raw Sugar — connecting Black Sea, LATAM and South Asian origins with global importing markets across South and South-East Asia.

Grains

Wheat (Milling, Feed), Corn / Maize, Soybeans. Origins: Russia, Ukraine, Brazil, Argentina. Basis: FOB, CFR, CIF. Cargo: 10,000 – 55,000 MT. Phytosanitary certificates on all shipments.

Raw Sugar

Raw Sugar ICUMSA 45 (VHP). Origins: Brazil (Santos), Thailand. Basis: FOB / CFR. Cargo: 5,000 – 25,000 MT. Quality: ICUMSA 45 max, Pol 99.3% min, Moisture 0.15% max.

Destination Markets

India, Bangladesh, Indonesia, Vietnam, Philippines, Sri Lanka — across the high-growth demand corridor where food security policy drives sustained import volumes.
