Complex trades need careful structuring. We assist clients in designing deal frameworks that mitigate risk and satisfy both parties' commercial objectives.

How It Works

Our Brokerage Process

A transparent, step-by-step process from first enquiry to successful trade completion.

01

Initial Enquiry & Briefing

You contact ECTRADE with your trade requirement — product specs, volume, timeline, incoterms preference and any specific counterparty criteria. We ask the right questions to fully understand your objectives.

02

Network Engagement & Counterparty Search

We activate our global network of buyers, sellers, traders, producers and off-takers to identify the best-fit counterparty for your transaction. All counterparties undergo preliminary vetting.

03

Introduction & Term Negotiation

We formally introduce both parties and facilitate commercial negotiations, advising on price, volume, delivery terms, and payment structures to bring both sides to agreement.

04

Contract and Documentation

We support the drafting and review of sale/purchase agreements, coordinate required documentation and assist with trade finance instrument setup if needed.

05

Execution & Delivery Monitoring

We remain engaged through cargo loading, shipping, discharge and final payment — serving as a neutral point of coordination for all parties until the transaction is fully closed.
