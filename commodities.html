Specifications

Key trade specifications

Coal Quality

We facilitate across the 5,500 – 6,300 kcal/kg GAR range. Standard quality parameters conforming to major benchmark specs.

  • Calorific Value: 5,500 – 6,300 kcal/kg GAR
  • Moisture: 8 – 25% (total)
  • Ash: 3 – 15% (as received)
  • Sulphur: 0.2 – 1.0% (as received)
  • Vessel: Handysize to Panamax

Metals Specifications

Iron ore, steel and copper specifications aligned to international industry-standard contracts and exchange references.

  • Iron Ore: 58 – 65% Fe, various size fractions
  • Steel Billets: 3SP / 5SP, JIS G3108
  • HRC: ASTM A1011 / JIS G3131
  • Copper Cathode: Grade A, BS EN 1978
  • Pricing: LME Cash / 3-Month

Fertilizer & Agri Quality

Fertilizer trades conform to IFA / IFIA standard requirements. Agri cargoes to buyers' specification sheets and phytosanitary requirements.

  • Urea: 46% N min, granular or prilled
  • DAP: 18% N, 46% P2O5 min
  • Sugar: ICUMSA 45 max (white / raw)
  • Wheat: Protein 10.5% min (milling grade)
  • Certification: SGS / Cotecna on shipment
