Quality Parameters

Key Trade Specifications

High-Thermal Coal

We broker across the 5,500–6,300 kcal/kg GAR range with standard quality parameters conforming to major benchmark specifications.

  • Ash: ≤ 12% (air-dried basis)
  • Total Moisture: ≤ 25%
  • Sulphur: ≤ 1.0%
  • HGI: ≥ 45 (typically 50–55)
  • Vessel range: Handysize to Panamax
  • Minimum cargo: 25,000 MT
Metals & Mining

Iron ore, steel and copper specifications aligned to industry-standard international contracts.

  • Iron Ore: 62% Fe fines / 62%+ lump
  • Steel Billets: 3SP / 5SP, Q195–Q235
  • HRC: ASTM / EN standards
  • Copper Cathode: LME Grade A, BS EN 1978
  • Pricing: LME, Platts SBB, SGX FEF1!
  • Typical lot: 1,000–30,000 MT
Fertilizers

Fertilizer trades conform to IFA/IFIA standard quality requirements and buyers' specification sheets.

  • Urea Granular: N ≥ 46.0%, Biuret ≤ 1.0%
  • Urea Prilled: N ≥ 46.0%, free-flowing spheres
  • DAP: P₂O₅ ≥ 46%, N ≥ 18%
  • Packaging: Bulk, Jumbo bags, 50kg bags
  • Cargo: 5,000 – 50,000 MT
  • Pricing: Argus, ICIS benchmark
