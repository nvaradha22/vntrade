Frequently Asked Questions

How does ECTRADE charge for its services?

ECTRADE earns a brokerage commission only upon successful completion of a trade. There are no upfront retainers or consultation fees. Our compensation is directly aligned with your outcome — we earn only when the deal closes.

Is my enquiry kept confidential?

All enquiries are treated with strict confidentiality. Your identity and commercial parameters are not shared with any third party without your explicit consent at each stage of the process.

How quickly will you respond to my enquiry?

We aim to respond to all enquiries within one business day. For time-sensitive requirements, please note urgency clearly in your message and we will prioritise accordingly.

Does ECTRADE trade for its own account?

No. ECTRADE is a pure broker and holds no principal commodity positions. This independence allows us to represent the interests of both sides of a transaction without any conflict of interest.

Which commodity sectors does ECTRADE cover?

Our current focus is: High-Thermal Grade Coal (5,500–6,300 kcal/kg GAR), Metals & Mining (Steel Products, Iron Ore, Copper), and Fertilizers & Agri Products (Urea, DAP, NPK, Grains, Sugar).
