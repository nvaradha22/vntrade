What We Facilitate

We do not hold title to any commodity. Principals transact directly. VN Trade provides the facilitation infrastructure that makes transactions complete.

Commodity 01

Coal

Thermal coal facilitation across key origin-destination corridors. Counterparty coordination, quality management and documentation advisory.

OriginsIndonesia · Russia · South Africa
DestinationsIndia (sub-continent) · GCC
GradesThermal · Steam Coal
Parcel SizeHandysize to Supramax
PrincipalsUtilities · Captive Power · Industrial

Facilitation Scope

  • Counterparty identification and qualification for both buy-side and sell-side mandates
  • Term sheet structuring — price basis, incoterms, quality specification, laycan windows
  • Quality inspection coordination — SGS, Bureau Veritas, Intertek appointment and oversight
  • LC documentation advisory — drafting guidance, amendment management, discrepancy resolution
  • Vessel nomination advisory and laycan compliance monitoring
  • Demurrage claim support and post-shipment settlement assistance

Principals Served

State Utilities Captive Power Plants Industrial End-Users Trading Houses Mining Offtake Holders
Compliance Note Sanctions screening applied on all counterparties, vessels and flag states. Russian-origin coal transactions subject to enhanced due diligence and applicable regulatory review.
Commodity 02

Fertilizers

Fertilizer mandate facilitation into South Asia and GCC markets. Pre-season procurement cycles and spot engagement windows.

ProductsUrea · DAP · MOP
DestinationsSouth Asia · GCC
BuyersDistributors · Cooperatives · State Buyers
SeasonsPre-Kharif · Pre-Rabi · Spot
Parcel SizeAs per mandate

Facilitation Scope

  • Seasonal mandate origination — pre-Kharif and pre-Rabi buying windows for South Asian buyers
  • Supplier qualification and grade specification alignment for Urea, DAP and MOP
  • Pricing basis advisory — CFR, CIF, FOB — and incoterm selection guidance
  • LC structuring support for state and cooperative buyer counterparties
  • Phytosanitary, origin and quality documentation coordination
  • GCC import permit advisory and regulatory compliance navigation

Principals Served

Fertilizer Distributors Agricultural Cooperatives State Procurement Bodies Blenders GCC Agri Importers
Compliance Note End-use documentation required for all fertilizer transactions. Precursor screening applied where applicable under applicable jurisdiction requirements.
Commodity 03

Metals

Steel and base metals facilitation serving GCC construction demand and Indian manufacturing principals. Quality-grade alignment and documentation coordination.

ProductsHRC · Steel Scrap · Copper · Aluminium
DestinationsIndia · GCC
BuyersManufacturers · Construction · Re-rollers
GradesAs per mandate specification
StandardsIS / ASTM / EN / JIS

Facilitation Scope

  • Grade and specification alignment between buyer technical requirements and seller mill certificates
  • HRC origin and mill qualification — Turkish, East Asian, CIS and domestic sources
  • Scrap grading advisory — HMS 1&2, Shredded, Zorba and non-ferrous grades
  • Copper and aluminium purity specification coordination and assay certification oversight
  • LC terms structuring for deferred payment and usance structures common in metals trade
  • BIS import licence advisory for regulated steel grades entering India

Principals Served

Steel Re-rollers Construction Groups Manufacturing Plants Scrap Processors GCC Project Buyers
CBAM Note Transactions involving steel and aluminium destined for European buyers are subject to CBAM (Carbon Border Adjustment Mechanism) requirements from 2026. Embedded carbon content documentation advisory available on request.
Commodity 04

Agri

Pulse and lentil facilitation into GCC, Southeast Asia and European markets. Origin qualification, phytosanitary coordination and buyer-seller alignment.

ProductsPulses · Lentils
OriginsCanada · Australia · India · Myanmar
DestinationsGCC · Southeast Asia · Europe
BuyersImporters · Processors · Retailers
Parcel SizeContainer to Bulk

Facilitation Scope

  • Origin qualification — Canadian, Australian, Indian and Myanmar pulse and lentil sources
  • Grade, moisture and admixture specification alignment between buyer and seller
  • Phytosanitary certificate and fumigation documentation coordination
  • Food safety compliance advisory for EU and GCC import requirements
  • Container and bulk logistics coordination across key discharge ports
  • Payment terms structuring — LC, DP, DA — based on counterparty profile

Principals Served

Commodity Importers Food Processors GCC Distributors EU Importers Retail Supply Chain
Compliance Note EU food safety regulations and GCC Standardization Organization (GSO) import requirements observed. Phytosanitary and residue testing documentation coordinated as part of facilitation scope.
