Common Questions

Frequently Asked

How does VN Trade earn its fees? +

VN Trade earns a facilitation commission only upon successful completion of a trade. There are no upfront retainers or consultation fees. Our compensation is directly aligned with your outcome — we earn only when the deal closes.

Is my enquiry kept confidential? +

All enquiries are treated with strict confidentiality. Your identity and commercial parameters are not shared with any third party without your explicit consent at each stage of the process.

How quickly will you respond? +

We aim to respond to all mandates within 48 hours. For time-sensitive requirements, please note urgency clearly in your message and we will prioritise accordingly.

Does VN Trade hold commodity positions? +

No. VN Trade is a pure facilitator and holds no principal commodity positions. This independence allows us to represent the interests of both sides of a transaction without any conflict of interest.

What commodities and corridors do you cover? +

Our current focus is Thermal Coal (5,500–6,300 kcal GAR), Metals and Mining (Iron Ore, Steel Products, Copper, Aluminium), Fertilizers (Urea, DAP, MOP, NPK) and Agri Products (Pulses, Lentils, Wheat, Corn, Raw Sugar). Primary corridors: GCC, India sub-continent, Southeast Asia and Europe. Contact us if your requirement falls outside these — our network is broad.

What happens after I submit a mandate? +

Your mandate is reviewed within 48 hours. If it falls within our facilitation scope, we will respond with a brief qualification call or written response to confirm parameters. No commitment is implied by this review. Engagement terms are agreed separately in writing before any facilitation activity begins.
